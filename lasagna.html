Lasagna


Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef until browned. Drain off any excess grease.
  3. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and egg
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used up.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until heated through and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
  11. Enjoy!
