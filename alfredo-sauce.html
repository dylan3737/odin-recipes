Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients:


  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:


  1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  2. Stir in the heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until slightly thickened.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  4. Serve immediately over your favorite pasta.
  5. Enjoy!
